Make an account to exchange positivity with strangers.

📝

Write

Write a kind message for a stranger. It could be:

  • a positive affirmation
  • a short poem
  • an inspiring quote
  • a word of advice
  • anything you want, as long as it's positive!

💌

Receive

Once you submit your message, you'll receive a message from a stranger, too. This message is unique, and will only be sent to you, unless you pass it on.

📬

Pass It On

View all your messages, add upvotes or downvotes, and forward your favorites along to other users.
